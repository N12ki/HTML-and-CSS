                        🍃 How to Care for you Gardan
        

                                
    
  • Improved  Air Quality:
 Plants play a crucial role in filtering and purifying the air. 
            They absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen through photosynthesis, 
                which helps improve indoor and outdoor air quality. 
        

        
    
        
  • Reduction:
    • Spending time around flowers and tending to them can have 
                a calming effect on the mind, reducing stress and anxiety levels.    
        
    
        
  • Community Engagement:
    • Flower gardening can bring communities together through shared gardening spaces, 
            events, and workshops, fostering a sense of belonging and collaboration.    
        
    
        
  • Seasonal Enjoyment:
    •  Different flowers bloom in different seasons, providing a 
                changing landscape throughout the year and adding excitement to gardening.
